Meet the team!

Andrew Brudnak

Hello, my name is Andrew Brudnak, and I’m passionate about software. Currently, I’m attending Lambda School studying full stack web development. In addition to the curriculum Lambda offers I’m also learning the Go programming language, I was drawn to the language since it was invented by luminaries in computer science. Whenever I’m not behind a keyboard my favorite things to do include: traveling and spending time with my family. My role for the Wanderlust project was creating the back-end system, I implemented this using Java and the Spring Framework. The database is PostgreSQL and it’s hosted on Heroku. It was a pleasure working with Diana Grams and Scott Prins, I hope you enjoy our application.
